Student certification grants access to a fast-track membership upgrade, allowing you to enjoy membership discounts. Each certification is valid for one year, after which recertification is required.

![](/images/student-verification.png)

If you are unable to complete the certification on the website, please contact our technical support or customer service for assistance.
