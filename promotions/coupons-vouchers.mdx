## Coupons

### Claiming

The entry point is on the right side of the homepage → Claim Coupons.

When claiming coupons, please confirm the scope of use for the coupons, which generally includes: 

- applicable to all products,
- available in certain regions,
- and available for certain products.

![](/images/claim-coupon.png)

Choose the coupons that are suitable for you.

### Usage

Eligible Billing Mode:

1. Discount and cash-back coupons can only be used for subscription plans ( including new subscriptions, switching from Pay-as-you-go to Subscription mode, and renewing existing subscriptions ).
2. If you are on a pay-as-you-go mode, you can switch to an subscription mode to use the coupon and then switch back before the subscription expires.

Maximum Discount:

Coupons typically have a maximum discount limit, such as $10 per unit per day.

## Vouchers

Vouchers have their own denominations and are used to offset the amount on specific service bills. The final interpretation rights belong to GPUhub.

### Referral Bonus

Referral bonus are issued to users who refer new users to GPUhub. The new users can use the vouchers to offset the amount on their service bills.

![](/images/referral-bonus.png)


<Steps>
<Step title="Copy Your Unique Referral Link">
Go to the “Your Referral Link” section and click the purple **Copy** button (or manually copy the full URL that contains your personal referral code).
</Step>
<Step title="Share the Link with Your Friends">
**Share the link** to your friends via WhatsApp, Telegram, WeChat, Discord, SMS, email, or any other platform. ⚠️ They must register using your exact link; otherwise, the referral will not be tracked and you will not receive any reward.
</Step>
<Step title="Your Friend Completes the Requirement">
Signs up through your link, makes their first deposit of $20 or more (or equivalent in other currencies)
</Step>
<Step title="You Get Rewarded Instantly">
Once the above conditions are met, you will **automatically receive** $6 (issued as a Voucher/Bonus) for that referral.
</Step>
</Steps>
