### Invoice Type

We offer two invoicing methods: 

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="1. Based on Bill Amount" icon="file-invoice-dollar">
    The amount of your order that has already been consumed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="2. Based on Recharge Amount" icon="credit-card">
    The amount that has been successfully paid online through recharge.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

The differences between them are as follows:

1. When invoicing based on recharges, you can issue an invoice immediately after recharging and customize the invoicing amount (with a minimum of USD10 per invoice).
2. When invoicing based on bill amounts, When invoicing based on bill amounts, invoices can only be issued after the subscribed or purchased period has ended.

### Apply for Invoice
To apply, please provide the information below to our [customer service](mailto:support@gpuhub.com) team.

```json
Name //individual's full legal name (match with your payment info)
Title //optional
Company / Organization Name //optional
Billing Address //country & city minimum
Contact Email //for receiving the invoice
User ID //obtained in GPUhub Console - Account - Profile
Transaction ID //obtained in GPUhub Console - Payment - Transactions
Invoice Currency //default USD
Invoice Amount //Minimum USD10 per invoice
```
We will respond **within 3 business days** and attach the PDF file to our email.
